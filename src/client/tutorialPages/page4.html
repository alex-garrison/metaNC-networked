Winning the Ultimate Game

To win the ultimate game of Ultimate Noughts and Crosses, you must achieve one of these conditions:

  1. Win three smaller grids in a row horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.
  2. Win three smaller grids in a vertical stack, one above the other.
  3. Win three smaller grids in a diagonal pattern, connecting from the top-left to the bottom-right or top-right to bottom-left.

Once you accomplish one of these conditions, you are the ultimate winner of Ultimate Noughts and Crosses!
