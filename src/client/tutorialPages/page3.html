Making Moves

Here's how you make moves in Ultimate Noughts and Crosses:

  1. Click on an empty cell in any of the smaller grids. Your symbol will appear there.
  2. Your opponent will be directed to the corresponding smaller grid where they must make their move.
  3. The game continues in this manner until one player wins three smaller grids in a row, column, or diagonal.

Remember, you can only make a move in the grid determined by your opponent's last move.
