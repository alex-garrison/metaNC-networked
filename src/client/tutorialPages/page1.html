Ultimate Noughts and Crosses

Ultimate Noughts and Crosses is a fun and strategic variation of the classic game. The goal is to win not just one, but three games of Noughts and Crosses!

Let's get started with the basic rules:

  • Objective: Win three games of Noughts and Crosses (3x3 grids) in a row, column, or diagonal to claim the ultimate victory.
  • How to Play: Click on a cell in any of the smaller grids to make your move. Your opponent will then make their move in the corresponding smaller grid.
  • Winning a Smaller Grid: To win a smaller grid, get three of your symbols (either noughts or crosses) in a row, column, or diagonal.
